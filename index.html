About Me

How My Journey As A Software developer started

I read an article about Software Engineers and how the percentage of women in this field are less and the percentage of females with color were really dissapointing. This career became so interesting to me after reading the article. I spoke to a mutual friend of mine who's name is Noah Addy about my sudden interest in Tech and to my surprise, he happens to be the Director of Engineers at SAP Concur, Bellevue. Even though I was ready to start my tech career but starting all over college with the little support I had was not an option for me. I learnt about coding bootcamp from my mutual friend Noah, he quickly scheduled me to meet up and connect with other developers who did bootcamp route. Their advices and experience really helped me to have a solid understanding of my new found career. I would want other women to have the confidence to pick up a career in Tech. I am extremely passionate about cultural diversity, leadership and mentorship.
