About Me

How My Journey As A Software developer started

I read an article about Software Engineers and how the percentage of women in this field are less and the percentage of females with color are really dissapointing. This career became an interest to me after reading the article. After a massive research and inquiries, I made up a firm decision to start a career path in tech through a bootcamp. I would want other women to have the confidence to pick up a career in Tech. I am extremely passionate about cultural diversity, leadership and mentorship.
